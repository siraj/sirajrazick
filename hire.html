Hire Me

I am available for software consultancy and system architecture design engagements. With over a decade of experience across open source desktop, browser engine, mobile, blockchain, and AI infrastructure projects, I can help your team design, build, and ship complex systems.

Software Consultancy

I provide hands-on consulting for teams that need deep technical expertise in any of the following areas:

  • Browser engine internals - V8, SpiderMonkey, WebKit, and Mozilla/Gecko performance work, rendering pipeline optimization, and JavaScript engine tuning.
  • Linux desktop & platform - GNOME, KDE, Wayland compositor protocols, display server bridges, and desktop integration work.
  • Mobile & multimedia - GStreamer pipelines, mobile OS platforms, and multimedia stack architecture.
  • Blockchain & Web3 - ledger system design, BTC-based protocols, and decentralized application architecture.
  • AI infrastructure - agent tooling, GPU orchestration, inference platform design, and agentic system architecture.
  • Code review & mentoring - reviewing critical codepaths, improving code quality, and upskilling your engineering team.

Architecture Design

I help organizations design scalable, maintainable system architectures from the ground up or refactor existing systems. My approach focuses on:

Service Description
Greenfield Architecture End-to-end system design for new products - from component breakdown and technology selection to data flow, deployment topology, and scaling strategy.
Architecture Review Audit of existing systems to identify bottlenecks, technical debt, and failure modes. Delivered as a written report with prioritized recommendations.
Performance Engineering Profiling and optimization of latency-sensitive systems - rendering pipelines, inference serving, network I/O, and concurrency bottlenecks.
Platform Design Design of internal developer platforms, CI/CD pipelines, release infrastructure, and observability stacks.
Distributed Systems Design of distributed ledgers, consensus mechanisms, replication strategies, and fault-tolerant service architectures.

Engagement Model

I work on a project or retainer basis, remote or on-site. Typical engagements include:

  • Short-term advisory - 1 to 4 weeks of focused consulting, architecture review, or technical due diligence.
  • Project engagements - 1 to 6 months of embedded work designing and building a specific system or subsystem.
  • Retainer arrangements - Ongoing advisory availability for teams that need a trusted architect on call.

All engagements begin with a free initial consultation to understand your requirements and determine whether I am the right fit. I will provide a written scope, timeline, and estimate before any work begins.
