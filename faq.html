Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What areas do you work in?

A: My work spans Linux desktop technologies, browser engines, mobile and multimedia, blockchain systems, and AI infrastructure. See the projects page for details.

Q: What browser engines have you worked on?

A: I have contributed to V8 (performance optimizations), SpiderMonkey (GObject Introspection), Mozilla/Gecko (CSS animations), WebKit (HTML5 video, WebRTC), and Google Chrome (e-ink rendering).

Q: What is PlexyDesk?

A: PlexyDesk is a desktop extension framework I developed that provides compositor-level desktop widgets and a protocol for desktop shell integration with Wayland.

Q: What is CloudGPU?

A: CloudGPU is an AI infrastructure and agent tooling platform I founded. It provides GPU compute resources and tooling for AI agent development and deployment.

Q: What is efinder.lk?

A: efinder.lk is an agentic fact-checking engine that uses AI agents to verify claims and provide sourced fact-checking.

Q: What is Hola Agent?

A: Hola Agent is an AI agent framework for building autonomous agents with tool-use capabilities, memory, and task planning.

Q: What blockchain projects have you worked on?

A: I have worked on Web3 systems, Bitcoin Core, and LiquidBTC / sidechain technology. My work focuses on ledger systems and protocol-level infrastructure.

Q: What was your role in the Nokia / MeeGo ecosystem?

A: I contributed to GStreamer, the Mozilla-based Nokia Browser, and the Nokia Harmattan / MeeGo mobile operating system. My work covered system-level components, UI framework integration, and multimedia.

Q: Why does this site look like it's from the 1990s?

A: Because it is. I believe in fast, readable, accessible web pages. No JavaScript, no tracking, no bloated frameworks. Just HTML and CSS. It works in any browser, including Lynx.

Q: How can I contact you?

A: See the contact page. Please read this FAQ first.
