efinder.lk: Agentic Fact-Checking with AI

2023-11-20

I recently released efinder.lk, an agentic fact-checking engine that uses AI to verify claims and detect misinformation.

The system works by breaking down a claim into searchable queries, gathering evidence from multiple sources, and then reasoning about the collected information to arrive at a verdict. This is all done autonomously by an AI agent.

Key design decisions:

  • Multi-source verification - the agent cross-references multiple sources before reaching a conclusion
  • Transparent reasoning - each step of the fact-checking process is logged and can be reviewed
  • Sinhala and Tamil support - built for the Sri Lankan context with local language processing

The project is open source and available on GitHub. I am actively looking for contributors who are interested in AI safety and misinformation research.
