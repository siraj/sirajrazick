efinder.lk: Agentic Fact-Checking with AI

2023-11-20

I recently released efinder.lk, a fact-checking engine that uses AI agents to verify claims and detect misinformation.

Here's how it works: the system breaks down a claim into searchable queries, gathers evidence from multiple sources, and then reasons about the collected information to arrive at a verdict. All of this happens autonomously, driven by an AI agent.

A few design decisions worth noting:

  • Multi-source verification — the agent cross-references multiple sources before reaching a conclusion
  • Transparent reasoning — each step of the fact-checking process is logged and can be reviewed
  • Sinhala and Tamil support — built for the Sri Lankan context with local language processing

The project is open source and on GitHub. I'm actively looking for contributors who are into AI safety and misinformation research.
